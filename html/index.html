ATiM - Advanced Tissue Management

About

The Canadian Tumour Repository Network (CTRNet) is a translational cancer research resource, funded by Canadian Institutes of Health Research, that furthers Canadian health research by linking cancer researchers with provincial tumour banks.

ATiM is a Biobank Information Management System (BIMS) designed and developed by CTRNet in conjunction with Canadian biobanks for the operation of biobanks. ATiM is released for free non-commercial use under GPL v3.


Installation

To view your installed version number open the Administration Tool and select ATiM Version from the first menu. ATiM is built on the CakePHP framework (www.cakephp.org).


Credits

ATiM is an open-source project development by leading tumour banks across Canada. For more information on our development team, questions, comments or suggestions please visit our website at http://www.ctrnet.ca.
